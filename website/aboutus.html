About Us

Welcome to ocean Fertilizers & Chemicals

Founded in 2014, "Ocean Fertilizer & Chemicals" has emerged as a reliable and renowned manufacturer in the field. We specialize in crafting a diverse range of products, including Zinc Sulphate Powder, Zinc Sulphate Heptahydrate, Copper Slag, and more. As a partnership-based company headquartered in Vadodara, Gujarat, India, we have established ourselves as a steadfast presence in the industry.

Our commitment to quality and excellence drives us to offer products in varying specifications, ensuring that we cater to the unique needs of our clients and achieve their complete satisfaction. With a focus on delivering nothing short of the best, we maintain a strong logistical framework that guarantees the timely delivery of our products.

Under the guidance and leadership of "Mr. Abhishek Ajmera," our esteemed partner, we have been able to cultivate a vast and loyal clientele across the country. His vision and direction have played a pivotal role in our growth and success, guiding us to become a trusted name in the realm of fertilizers and chemicals.

At Ocean Fertilizer & Chemicals, we pride ourselves on our dedication to quality, customer satisfaction, and innovation. As we continue on our journey, we remain committed to upholding these values and furthering our reputation as a leading manufacturer in the industry.
